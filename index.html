Our Support Priorities

Since the Russia's invasion of Ukraine in 2014, the Association has been focused on raising awareness of the war in America and providing medical and humanitarian aid to civilians, non-profit organizations and hospitals, wounded soldiers, and families of soldiers who died defending Ukraine.

  • Deliver medical supplies to hospitals in Ukraine
    • Provide neonatal incubators for newborns
  • Provide civilian defenders with body armor
  • Support civilians with life-saving medicine
  • Help displaced families and orphans

About Us

Ukrainian Association of Washington State is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. The organization does not have paid employees - all operations are based solely on volunteer support.

Since its foundation in 1971 the Association has organized numerous cultural and political events, sponsors Ukrainian culture organizations and programs, promotes awareness nd relations between US and Ukraine. Our largest programs include:

  • Annual Ukraine's Independence day celebration and annual birthday celebration of Ukrainian bard Taras Shevchenko
  • Organized Ukrainian language classes in UW in 1973 and continues to sponsor Ukrainian Studies
  • Sponsors Ukrainian dance group Barvinok and chorus Serpanok
